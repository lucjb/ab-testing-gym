A/B Testing Gym: Exercise Your Decision Power!

Ready to become an A/B testing expert?

The A/B Testing Gym is a fun and engaging way to learn and practice decision-making skills with realistic experiments.

How to Play

Analyze experiments, make decisions, score and learn!

  1. You will be challenged to analyze hypothetical experiments and make three critical decisions:
    • Evaluate if the experiment is trustworthy
    • Choose the best decision based on the results
    • Determine the next steps to maximize impact
  2. Each experiment is based on a real-world scenario and is designed to test your decision-making skills. What you will see:
    • Experiment Design: Details about the experiment design, such as alpha, beta, sample size, etc.
    • Experiment Progress: How much data has been collected and how much is left to collect
    • Experiment Results: Full results of the experiment including metrics, daliy charts and statistical analyses
  3. Get instant feedback on your decisions and learn from each choice
  4. You'll face all sorts of traps! Can you spot them and adjust your decisions?

What is your Decision? Based on the experiment results, choose whether to:
  • Keep Base: Stick with the original version
  • Keep Variant: Implement the new version
  • Keep Running: Continue the experiment to gather more data
Consider statistical significance, business impact, and confidence intervals when making your decision.
What's your Follow Up? Choose the next steps based on your decision:
  • Celebrate: Successfully implemented a winning variant
  • Iterate: Make improvements to the winning variant
  • Validate: Run additional tests to confirm results
  • Fix & Rerun: Address issues and restart the experiment
  • None: No immediate action needed
Consider business impact and resource allocation when choosing follow-up actions.
